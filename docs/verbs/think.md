I. ***have a particular opinion, belief, or idea about someone or something***<br>
*"she thought that nothing would be the same again"*{.gray}

1\. *used in questions to express anger or surprise*<br>
*"what do you think you're doing?"*{.gray}

2\. *used in speech to reduce the force of a statement, or to politely suggest or refuse something*<br>
*"I thought we could go out for a meal"*{.gray}

II. ***direct one's mind towards someone or something; use one's mind actively to form connected ideas***<br>
*"he was thinking about Colin"*{.gray}

1\. *have a particular mental attitude or approach*<br>
*"he thought like a general"*{.gray}

2\. *have sufficient foresight or awareness to do something*<br>
*"I hadn't thought to warn Rachel about him"*{.gray}

3\. *imagine or expect (an actual or possible situation)*<br>
*"think of being paid a salary to hunt big game!"*{.gray}
